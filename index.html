About

Thank you for visiting Hawkmatix!

We are all about providing tools to traders without restrictions. That is why we publish free add-on software for trading platforms such as NinjaTrader. Our goal is to provide quality open source technical analysis software available to everyone.

We are interested in your ideas! If you have an idea for a new project, and would like it to be developed in an open source setting, send us an email.
